### Short description
```

```

### Full description
```
⛔ al-Ghirbal — The Sieve

Interactive trilingual app (Arabic / English / French) based on the book "This is Not Islam" by Sheikh Mohammed al-Ghazali (1917–1996).

📚 About the book

The book sifts through practices and customs falsely attached to Islam. Six chapters: three establish what Islam truly is (objectives of Sharia, the concept of innovation, Islamic thought), and three reveal innovations in creed, worship, and customs.

✨ Features

• 15 sifted myths (creed / worship / customs)
• 8 "Truth" cards
• "Women's Rights in Islam" section
• 17-question self-assessment quiz with verdicts
• Three languages: Arabic, English, French
• Three color themes
• Reading mode, audio narrator, full RTL Arabic support
• Works offline — no internet required

🕌 Disclaimer

I am not a scholar. This is a humble effort by a Muslim parent to share Sheikh al-Ghazali's wisdom interactively. Content is derived from the book and trusted Islamic sources. This is not a fatwa. For in-depth religious questions, please consult qualified scholars.

📖 Sources

• "This is Not Islam" — Sheikh Mohammed al-Ghazali
• The Holy Quran and authentic Sunnah
• Works of established scholars in jurisprudence and creed

May Allah have mercy on Sheikh al-Ghazali and reward him 🤲

—
Powered by workshop-diy.org
```
